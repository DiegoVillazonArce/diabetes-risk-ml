| D-016 | 2026-07-09 | Accepted | Select the P5 `HistGradientBoostingClassifier` candidate (library defaults, fixed seed) as the primary MVP model. | P5 compared Dummy, Logistic Regression, a `class_weight="balanced"` Logistic Regression variant, and `HistGradientBoostingClassifier` on the same P3 splits with the documented metric protocol. The tree-based candidate leads the primary ranking metric, PR-AUC (0.423 vs. 0.394 for Logistic Regression and 0.393 for the balanced variant), also has the strongest ROC-AUC (0.827 vs. 0.819/0.820) and best default-threshold positive-class precision (0.563), and keeps a small train/test PR-AUC gap (0.032, under the 0.10 overfitting limit). The balanced variant has the best default-threshold recall (0.760) and F1 (0.441), but does not improve PR-AUC or ROC-AUC; honest probability ranking matters more for a risk-estimation app, and threshold/calibration work belongs to P8. | Later phases (P6 Streamlit serving, P8 calibration, P9 SHAP) build on `HistGradientBoostingClassifier`. Artifact serialization follows the D-017 timing with the D-010 `joblib` format, and D-013 still governs deployment distribution. The low default-threshold recall of the selected unweighted model is a documented limitation for the P8 threshold analysis. |
